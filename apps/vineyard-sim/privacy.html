Privacy Policy

How Vineyard Sim handles your information.

Updated · Apr 22, 2026 Plain language
I.

Overview

Vineyard Sim is a single-player simulation game. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app accesses, stores, and transmits.

II.

Data collection

The app does not require an account, login, or registration to play. It does not collect your name, email address, or other personally identifiable information.

Third-party services integrated into the app may collect limited data as described below.

  • Advertising (Google AdMob) — the app displays ads to support free gameplay. Google AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as the Advertising ID), approximate location, and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings. See the Google Privacy Policy.
  • In-App Purchases (RevenueCat) — the app uses RevenueCat to manage optional in-app purchases. RevenueCat may collect anonymous device identifiers and purchase transaction data to process and verify purchases. No personal information is shared. See the RevenueCat Privacy Policy.
III.

Local storage

Game save data — your vineyard progress, settings, and achievements — is stored locally on your device using standard platform storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

IV.

Network usage

The app uses Google Fonts for typography, which may make network requests to download font files on first launch. See the Google Fonts Privacy FAQ.

The app also makes network requests to load ads (Google AdMob) and to verify in-app purchases (RevenueCat). All gameplay is fully offline and does not require an internet connection.

V.

Advertising

The app displays interstitial ads at natural pauses in gameplay (e.g., between game screens). You can remove all interstitial ads permanently through an in-app purchase.

Ads are served by Google AdMob. Google may use device identifiers and usage data to personalize ads. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device's privacy settings (Settings › Privacy › Apple Advertising on iOS).

VI.

Children's privacy

The app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages.

VII.

Third-party services

Vineyard Sim integrates the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — ad serving. Privacy policy.
  • RevenueCat — in-app purchase management. Privacy policy.
  • Google Fonts — typography. Privacy FAQ.
VIII.

Changes to this policy

If this policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
